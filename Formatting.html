Rajasthan Patrika:

Corona News:

Hindusthan me corona case's ki sankya 8 lakh ke paar. Badthe corona ko dekh kar sarkar ne lockdown badane ka liya fasela. Ab 14 din ka hoga or lockdown sabi niyam honge lagu. Bihar me corona ki rafftar hui tej ak din me mile record 10000 marij. Bihar corona ke mamle me 3 isthan per pahucha. Bihar me ab tak corona marijo ki sankya 50000 hajar ke paar. Corona ke saath-saath bihar me pahadi bahad ki maar. Bihar ke 08 jilo me huva pani ka bahrav. Bihar sarkar ne le aa kai gumbir gambir fasele.

Bharat Chine Sima Vivad News:

Bharat or chine ke bich badthe sima vivad ko lacher raksha mantri rajnath singh ne chiff defence of staff vipin rawat or army chiff m.m. narvane ke saath ki bhatk. Raksha mantri rajnath singh ki chiff defence or army chiff ke saath (2)2 ganthe ki hui bhatk. Bhatk me li aa gae kai aaham fasele.
